Social Media Etiquette Checklist


Do's	Don'ts
Privacy First: Regularly review and update your privacy settings.	Oversharing: Never post sensitive info like your home address or OTPs.
Think Twice: Assume anything you post is permanent and searchable.	Cyberbullying: Avoid engaging in or supporting online harassment.
Be Professional: Use professional profile photos for LinkedIn and GitHub.	Fake News: Don't share information without verifying the source first.
Give Credit: Always tag or credit creators when sharing their work.	Offensive Language: Avoid using slang or language that could be seen as biased.
Secure Accounts: Enable Two-Factor Authentication (2FA) on all platforms.	Unprofessional Tags: Don't tag others in photos without their permission.
