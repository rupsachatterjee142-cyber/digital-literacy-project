### 🛡️ Cyber Crime Prevention & Safety Guide

| **Safety Category** | **Actionable Tip** |
| :--- | :--- |
| **UPI Security** | **Never** enter your PIN to receive money. A PIN is only for debits/sending. |
| **Link Safety** | Avoid clicking on shortened URLs or "Cashback" links sent via SMS/WhatsApp. |
| **Identity Check** | Always verify the caller's identity. Banks will never ask for your PIN or OTP over the phone. |
| **Account Defense** | Enable Two-Factor Authentication (2FA) on your Gmail, LinkedIn, and social media. |
| **Transaction Limits** | Set a daily UPI transaction limit in your app to minimize potential loss from scams. |
| **Public Wi-Fi** | Avoid accessing banking apps or sensitive data while connected to free public Wi-Fi. |
| **App Security** | Only download UPI or banking apps from the official Google Play Store or Apple App Store. |
| **Immediate Action** | If defrauded, immediately call **1930** or report at **www.cybercrime.gov.in**. |
